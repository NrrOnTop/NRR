What is NRR?

NRR means No Realm Remains, is a relentless Minecraft team with a mission to dominate. Led by vortexghost, NRR takes over realms, uncovers exploits, and leaves a haunting mark across the Minecraft community. Dare to join, or watch NRR’s legend grow.

How to Join Team NRR?

Want to be a part of the chaos? To join NRR, head to the Contact link in the menu and join our Discord. Once inside, follow the instructions to open a ticket. But beware—there’s no going back...
